How to buy $DDY

1
Get a Solana wallet

Download a Solana wallet like Phantom or Backpack. Create a new wallet, back up your seed phrase and keep it offline and private.

2
Fund with SOL

Buy SOL on an exchange and send it to your wallet address. You’ll use SOL to swap into $DDY and pay network fees.

3
Swap for $DDY

Once the contract address is live, paste it into a Solana DEX (Raydium, Jupiter, etc.), confirm it is the correct token and swap your SOL into DDY.

4
Join the pack

Add DDY to your wallet watchlist, join the community channels, and help build the culture with memes, feedback and good energy.
